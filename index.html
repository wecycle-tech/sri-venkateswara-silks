Welcome to Sri Venkateswara Silks

About Us

Sri Venkateswara Silks has been a hallmark of excellence in the world of textiles since our establishment in 1980. What began as a modest venture with just eight handlooms has evolved into a leading manufacturer of pure Kanchipuram silk sarees, boasting over 100 looms dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of handloom weaving.

Our Heritage

We take pride in our own handloom manufacturing, crafting each saree with precision and artistry to honor our rich textile heritage.
