Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Read on to learn about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us when you visit our store, such as your name, contact details, and purchase history. No online data collection or transactions occur as we only accept direct orders in-store.

2. Use of Information

We use your information solely for managing orders, assisting with inquiries, and providing a personalized customer experience. We do not sell or share your information with third parties without your consent.

3. Data Security

Your personal information is securely stored and managed. We have implemented security measures to protect against unauthorized access or misuse of your data.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy policy or need to access or update your information, please contact us directly at our store or call us at +91 7411598103.
